About Us

T2 - CITY APARTMENT

THE BEST CITY APARTMENT IN VLORA

Set in Vlorë, 1.3 km from Vjetër Beach and 1.9 km from Vlore Beach, T2 city apartments offers accommodation with free WiFi, air conditioning and access to a garden .

Each unit has a balcony, a kitchen with a fridge, a dining area and a seating area with a flat-screen TV, while the private bathroom includes a bidet and slippers.

Independence Square is 1.7 km from the aparthotel, while Kuzum Baba is 1.9 km from the property. The nearest airport is Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 150 km from T2 city apartments.
